About the Project

The SESD Exam Portal is a web-based application designed to simplify the process of conducting online examinations. It was developed as a project for the [Subject Name] course to demonstrate a practical application of web development technologies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a secure, reliable, and user-friendly platform that bridges the gap between traditional examination methods and modern digital solutions. We aim to reduce the administrative burden on professors while offering students a distraction-free testing environment.

Key Features

  • Role-based access for Students and Professors.
  • Easy-to-use exam creation tools for professors.
  • Secure test-taking interface for students.
  • Instant grading and result generation for objective questions.
